ABOUT "EXPLORE TBILISI"

At "Explore Tbilisi", we're your local friends on a mission to make your time in our vibrant city unforgettable. Let's go beyond the tourist trail, discovering hidden gems and sharing the stories that make Tbilisi unique. Join us for an adventure that's all about connecting with the heart and soul of the city.

WHAT IS "EXPLORE TBILISI"?

Explore Tbilisi is your gateway to an authentic and immersive experience in the heart of Georgia's capital. We are more than just a travel service; we are your local companions, passionate about unveiling the soul of Tbilisi. Our mission is to curate unique and personalized journeys, allowing you to discover the city's hidden treasures, vibrant culture, and delectable cuisine. Whether you're a seasoned traveler or a first-time visitor, Explore Tbilisi is here to guide you on a memorable adventure, ensuring you forge a genuine connection with this enchanting city.

We created "Explore Tbilisi" out of our passion for sharing the authentic and lesser-known aspects of this remarkable city.
